Why MySC

Purpose-built for life at sea, where networks are hostile, filtered, and constantly changing.

Private by design

We never log your browsing activity, the sites you visit, or the content of your traffic — and we never sell your data.

Works on any ship network

Tuned to get through demanding maritime connections — VSAT, Starlink and heavily-filtered crew Wi-Fi.

Best route, automatically

MySC probes every server and port in parallel and connects through the fastest reachable one — every time, on every network.

Split tunneling

Choose exactly which apps use the secure connection and which use the ship's normal internet — in two simple modes.

One tap to connect

No manual port scanning, no fiddly setup. Open the app, tap once, and you're protected.

Encrypted end to end

All traffic between your device and our servers is encrypted, so it can't be read on the networks in between.
